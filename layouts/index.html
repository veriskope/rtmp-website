RTMP

Real Time Messaging Protocol (RTMP) was designed to support very low-latency for interactive, multiparty applications with seamless integration of live or recorded video, as well as broadcast streaming, video on demand (VOD) use cases.

Get started

About the Protocol

RTMP Technology is the best video/audio stream protocol supporting the following features:

  • Live and on-demand
  • Low-latency
  • Streaming
  • Interactivity
  • Two-way (mux, demux)
  • Audio/Video/Data channels

De-Facto Standard

RTMP is the primary standard all major live streaming services support, including but not limited to:

  • YouTube
  • Facebook
  • Twitch
  • Mux
  • Wirecast
  • XSplit
